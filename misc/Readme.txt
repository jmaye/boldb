* Description: This video is a supplementary material to the paper "Bayesian
               On-line Learning of Driving Behaviors". It displays an example of
               the system in a real city environment and helps to better
               understand the presented concepts. A typical scenario of a car
               approaching a traffic light is shown.

* Player Information: This video is encoded in MPEG4 format and should be
                      readable by most of the available video players.
